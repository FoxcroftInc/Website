About Foxcroft

Investment, development, and management.

At Foxcroft, we are committed to providing exceptional investment, development and commercial property management services with a focus on personalized attention and seamless execution. As a regionally based firm, we specialize in investing and managing of commercial real estate, offering a diverse portfolio of apartments, offices, and retail spaces throughout the greater Boston area.

Foxcroft has a long history of delivering exceptional returns to our investors and superior management for our tenants. Foxcroft owns and manages well-located residential, office, and retail properties throughout Greater Boston, New Hampshire, and now in Georgia.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Whether you are a tenant looking for the ideal space, a broker seeking strategic partnerships, or a property owner in need of an experienced joint venture partner, Foxcroft delivers prompt, professional, and personalized service. Our hands-on approach ensures that you work directly with key decision-makers, allowing for swift execution and optimal returns.

With over 30 years of successful management and investment expertise, we have built a strong reputation for providing prime, well-maintained commercial properties that foster business success. For our select group of investors, our consistent track record of superior returns speaks for itself.
